Obstacle Avoiding Wall Climbing Robot


A Wall Climbing Robot is a robot designed for certain tasks which require the extension of the aspect of reach which is limited for humans. The parts required for assembling a wall climbing robot are very specific in nature. These include motors, suction cups, vacuum pumps etc. Regarding the parts included there are a certain advantages and limitations which limit the usage potential and create alternatives for the existing system.
